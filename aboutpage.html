Basic Rules of Baseball

  1. Two teams play 9 innings
  2. The pitcher stands on the mound and throws the ball to the batter
  3. The batter stands at home plate trying to hit the ball
  4. The pitch count is how many balls the pitcher has thrown in the at-bat
  5. The batter can strike out when they have 3 strikes
  6. The batter walks if the pitcher misses the zone and the batter does not swing 4 times
  7. The team needs 3 outs to end the inning
  8. The home team will bat last
